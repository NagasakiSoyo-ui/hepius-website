与 PD-1 抑制剂联用的差异化单抗

HP-001 是一款针对新型免疫抑制通路的人源化单克隆抗体，旨在与 PD-1 抑制剂联合使用，扩大免疫检查点疗法的获益人群。基于赫匹欧斯反向转化平台对 PD-1i 耐受/难治人群的洞察，HP-001 拥有差异化的科学假说与定位。候选分子已完成抗体发现、人源化改造及体内外活性评价，正在推进临床前候选 (PCC) 阶段后续工作。

PRAME TCR Platform: Dual Modality Therapy & Diagnostics

HP-002 is a soluble native T-cell receptor specifically recognizing PRAME₄₂₅₋₄₃₃ in complex with HLA-A*02:01. Built on Hepius's proprietary TCR discovery and engineering platform, HP-002 is being developed in two parallel modalities: a TCR-CD3 bispecific T-cell engager (TCE) for therapy, and a radiolabeled diagnostic agent for SPECT/CT imaging.

Dual Modality Strategy

Therapy — TCR-CD3 T-Cell Engager (TCE)

Three TCR-CD3 bispecific formats (002BsAb01/02/03) engineered with varying CD3 engagement geometry and valency. The lead candidate fully activates T cells in vitro and induces potent antigen-specific killing of PRAME-positive Hs695T tumor cells — without requiring TCR affinity enhancement.

Diagnostics — Radiolabeled SPECT/CT Imaging

¹²⁵I-labeled PR2-Fc-LALAPG variant demonstrates antigen-dependent tumor accumulation in PRAME-positive Hs695T xenograft models, with peak uptake at ~24 hours post-injection. Single-chain TCR variant (scPRC2) showed faster tumor uptake at ~4 hours with rapid clearance.

Platform Extension

The same TCR discovery platform has identified MAGE-A4₂₃₀₋₂₃₉/HLA-A*02:01-specific TCRs (KD = 9.18 × 10⁻⁷ M), expanding the pipeline to additional HLA-restricted intracellular tumor antigens.

A Dual-Modality Platform Targeting ALPP/ALPPL2 (ADC + TCE)

HP-004 is a platform spanning antibody-drug conjugate (ADC) and bispecific T-cell engager (TCE) modalities, targeting ALPP and ALPPL2 — alkaline phosphatase isozymes highly expressed in ovarian, endometrial, gastric, and non-small cell lung cancers, with restricted expression in normal tissues. HP-004 antibodies specifically bind ALPP/ALPPL2 without cross-reactivity to ALPI or ALPL, minimizing on-target off-tumor liability. Lead candidate: HY-X7514 (data presented at AACR 2026).

  • — IC₅₀ ~248 pM in ALPP/ALPPL2-overexpressing tumor cell lines
  • — Enhanced internalization efficiency over reference antibodies (Seagen)
  • — Near-complete tumor regression at 3 mg/kg (BiW × 2) in NCI-H1651 xenograft model
  • — Complete tumor regression in 6 of 8 treated mice
  • — Bystander killing activity in ALPP/ALPPL2-positive / ALPP/ALPPL2-negative co-culture models

Detailed data presented at AACR Annual Meeting 2026, Poster #6776.
