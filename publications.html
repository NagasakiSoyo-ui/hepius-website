Utilize a Soluble T-cell Receptor (TCR) Targeting Tumor Intracellular Antigen PRAME to Develop Therapeutic and Radioactive Diagnostic Agents for PRAME-positive Tumors

We established a proprietary TCR discovery platform for rapid identification of PRAME₄₂₅₋₄₃₃/HLA-A*02:01-specific TCRs without affinity enhancement. HP004, a Novel Antibody-Drug Conjugate Targeting Alkaline Phosphatases ALPP and ALPPL2 in Solid Tumors

ALPP and ALPPL2 are highly expressed in ovarian, endometrial, gastric, and non-small cell lung cancers, with restricted expression in normal tissues. HP-004 is a novel ADC dual-targeting ALPP/ALPPL2, conjugated with MMAE at an average DAR of ~4, demonstrating high selectivity and enhanced internalization with an IC₅₀ of ~248 pM. In the NCI-H1651 xenograft lung cancer model, HP-004 achieved near-complete tumor regression at 3 mg/kg (BiW × 2), with complete tumor regression in 6 of 8 mice.
